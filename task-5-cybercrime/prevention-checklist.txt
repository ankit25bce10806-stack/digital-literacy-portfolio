
‘Stay Safe Online’ Prevention Checklist

Prevention Tips for College Students :

- Use strong and unique passwords for every account.
- Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible.
- Avoid clicking on unknown links or downloading suspicious files.
- Keep social media accounts private.
- Never share UPI PIN, OTP, or bank login details with anyone.
- Update your smartphone, apps, and antivirus regularly because updates have resolved security issues.
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for banking or any personal logins.
- Report any suspicious online activity immediately.
- Think before sharing personal photos or any personal information online.
- Verify the authenticity of payment requests before approving them.
